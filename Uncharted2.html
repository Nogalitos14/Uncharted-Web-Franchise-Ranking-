Why Uncharted Among Thieves is placed second in the list

Uncharted 2 takes place 2 years after the first and starts the game with Nathan Drake dangling from a train about to fall off of a cliff. The main cast and more new main characters make a plan to search for the Cinamani stone and Shambala while again being targeted by a war criminal. This game is placed second to first in the list because of the amazing storyline and gameplay with captivating scenes. Uncharted 2 is a game that can immerse you in the story even if you haven't played the first.
